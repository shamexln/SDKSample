void S31::Adt::Impl::Device::notify ( const SubscriptionId &  subscription,
const PatientData &  patient 
)
overridevirtual

Notify the subscriber about changed patient data.

The product has to call this if there are changed patient for this subscription. The relation between the subscription and the patient are known to the product through a prior call to Service::subscriptionStarted(). The product has to store this relation and can therefore determine later those subscriptions that have to be notified.

Note that this call doesn't guarantee that the subscriber will be notified i.e. the subscriptions may be gone meanwhile. The call will have no effect in this case.
