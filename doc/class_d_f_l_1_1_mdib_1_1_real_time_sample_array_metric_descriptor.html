The resolution of the means to determine the metric's value.

◆ samplePeriod()

const Duration & DFL::Mdib::RealTimeSampleArrayMetricDescriptor::samplePeriod ( ) const

The sample period of the real-time sample array.

I.e., how often waveform samples are generated. The sample period is always given as a period between samples, e.g., 5 milliseconds.
