pure virtual

Notifcation that a client started a subscription to get updates regarding the given patient ID.

The product needs to store the relation between the subscription id and the patient id to notify about patient changes. The subscription IDs are unique (one Id will be notified here only once). The patient ID could be subscribed multiple times, e.g. multiple devices at a workplace have the same patient.
